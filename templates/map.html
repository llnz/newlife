Find My New Zealand

Find My New Zealand is a web tool to help you find where you want to live in New Zealand.

Whether you're a newly arrived migrant or already living in New Zealand and looking to move, Find My New Zealand will use your personal preferences to help you decide where to settle.

Data from:

  • StatsNZ, Digital Boundary Files
  • Dept Building and Housing, Geometric Mean Rents
